About Me

Biography

Muhammed Afsal Hudawi is a dedicated educator with dual postgraduate degrees in Islamic Studies and English Literature. With over eight years of teaching experience, he has developed a passion for fostering critical thinking and professionalism among learners. His journey began at Darul Huda Islamic University, where he honed his expertise in Islamic Jurisprudence and Human Sciences. Later, he expanded his horizons at the University of Calicut, mastering English Language and Literature.

Throughout his career, Muhammed has authored books on parenting and the integration of AI tools in education, showcasing his commitment to modernizing teaching methodologies. He has also played pivotal roles in curriculum development, instructional design, and mentoring, aiming to create innovative learning environments that leverage technology for academic growth.

His career goal is to contribute meaningfully to education, inspiring students and educators alike to embrace knowledge and professionalism.
